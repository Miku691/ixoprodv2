GK & Quiz About Odisha's First Person, Event and More

Welcome to ixoQuiz's special feature on Odisha's remarkable firsts GK and Quiz! In this post, we will have the rich content of Odisha's history, highlighting the trailblazing personalities, landmark events, and pioneering achievements that have shaped the state's cultural and historical landscape.

Whether you're a history enthusiast, a quiz lover, or just curious about Odisha's unique contributions, this post promises to be both informative and engaging.

1 - Who is the first chief minister of Odisha?

Show Description
Harekrushna Mahatab (born name Harekrushna Das) was the leader of the Indian National Congress and a notable figure in the Indian independence movement. He served as the Chief Minister of Odisha from 1946 to 1950 and again from 1956 to 1961.

2 - Who is the first IAS officer from Odisha?

Show Description
Ram Chandra Patra, IAS (retd.), was the first Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer from Kalahandi district, Odisha. He was born in Dharamgarh on April 24, 1919, and was known for his contributions as a bureaucrat, social worker, and administrator.

3 - Who is the first governor of Odisha?

Show Description
Sir John Austen Hubback, KCSI, was a British administrator in India who served as the first Governor of Odisha. He was educated at Winchester College and King's College, Cambridge, and entered the Indian Civil Service in 1902.

4 - What is the first capital of Odisha?

Show Description
Cuttack was the first capital of Odisha. It is one of the oldest cities in India, founded by King Ananga Bhimadev-III.

5 - What is the first university of Odisha?

Show Description
Utkal University, established in 1943, is the oldest university in Odisha and the 17th oldest university in India.

6 - What is the first newspaper in Odisha?

Show Description
Utkala Deepika, the first Odia printed newspaper, was started on August 4, 1866, by Gourishankar Ray and Babu Bichitrananda Das. August 4 is celebrated as Odia Journalism Day.

7 - Who is the first Odia pilot?

Show Description
Giribala Mohanty was the first female airwoman from Odisha and the first to have a license to fly a private aircraft.

8 - Who is the first IGP(Inspector General of Police) officer in Odisha?

Show Description
J.E. Pearman was the first Inspector General of Police (IGP) of Odisha after India gained independence in 1947.

9 - Who is the first scientist of Odisha?

Show Description
Dr. Pranakrushna Parija, a renowned botanist and Padma Bhushan awardee, is considered the first modern scientist from Odisha. He made significant contributions to agricultural development in the state of Odisha.

10 - What is the name of the first Odia film?

Show Description
Sita Bibaha, released on April 28, 1936, was the first Odia film, directed by Mohan Sundar Deb Goswami.

11 - What is the first college of Odisha?

Show Description
Ravenshaw College, established in 1868, is the first college in Odisha and one of the oldest in India.

12 - Who is the first freedom fighter of Odisha?

Show Description
Jayee Rajguru (1739 - 1806) is considered one of the first freedom fighters of Odisha and the first martyr to fight against the British East India Company.
